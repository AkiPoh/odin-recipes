Pork Tenderloin Strips Marinade - Recipe

Yo Xiang Pork - Recipe

Step 1

  • About 250g pork tenderloin strips

Put the pork strips in a bowl.

Step 2

  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 2 tbsp water

Mix together in a separate bowl, then add to the meat and mix.

Step 3

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 pinch white pepper
  • 1 pinch chili flakes

Add and mix into the meat.

Step 4

  • 1 tbsp potato starch
  • 1 tbsp water

Mix together in a separate bowl, then add to the meat and mix.

Step 5

  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil (or canola oil)
Pour over the meat.

Step 6

  • About 1 tbsp rapeseed (or canola oil)

Add the oil to a pan and heat to medium-high heat.

Fry the meat in the hot pan for about 2 minutes 30 seconds.

Raise the heat higher at the beginning to ensure the pan stays hot from the start. Remember to stir constantly at the beginning while frying so the marinade doesn't burn. Immediately after frying, transfer from the pan to rest on a plate covered with a lid.

Enjoy!
